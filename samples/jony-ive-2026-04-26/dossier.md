# OSINT Dossier — Sir Jonathan "Jony" Ive

## Summary
Sir Jonathan Paul Ive (b. 1967, London) is a British-American industrial designer, formerly Apple's Chief Design Officer (responsible for the iMac, iPod, iPhone, iPad, MacBook line), who left Apple in 2019 to found the creative collective **LoveFrom**, headquartered in San Francisco. In **May 2025**, LoveFrom announced a roughly **$6.5 B merger / partnership** with OpenAI's hardware venture **io** (co-founded with Sam Altman), and Ive is now leading design across OpenAI's AI-hardware program in addition to LoveFrom's existing client work (Ferrari, Airbnb, et al.). He remains based in San Francisco.
